Rock-Paper-Scissors game

Please design a computer program for a person to play Rock-Paper-Scissors game against computer. Assume the number of steps of the staircase is 3 and let the computer be your opponent. Play the game as you cast your figure at each round and find who is the winner of this game. Display the result of each round, and finally show who wins the game and report the total number of rounds in the game. Design the user input for Rock, Scissors, and Paper to be ‘R’, ‘S’ and ‘P’, respectively.
